Daniel Shen

Hi there!

My name is Daniel and I am a Cincinnati native - born and raised. For college, I left all that I knew to study Economics and Mathematics at Yale. My original intent with this website was to create a place where I could share my thoughts, catalog my experiences, and learn as much as possible.

Lesson one: building a website from scratch with no prior experience is surprisingly non-intuitive. Luckily, I have the backing of a few of the smartest, funniest, and kind-hearted people I know: my support network of family and friends. With their gratuitous help and understanding, I now possess the nascent beginnings of a blossoming website and the motivation necessary to keep plodding forward with this project.

As I continue to develop this site, please feel free to reach out with any questions, comments, or concerns (but preferably not with any bills or lawsuits). In other words, please tell me if I'm doing something wrong or if you come across anything that hasn't been cited properly. I'd also appreciate suggestions on how to improve. Otherwise, I hope you enjoy!

- Daniel
