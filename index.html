Hello!

I'm Daniel, and I was born and raised in Cincinnati. I attended Yale Unversity where I studied Economics and Mathematics [1]. In college, I was a Richard U. Light Fellow [2], a member of the Yale Advisory Committee on Investor Responsibility [3], and the leader of the Dwight Hall Socially Responsible Investment Fund [4]. I also served as a course development assistant for the Yale Mathematics Department [5], a peer tutor for the Yale Science and Quantitative Reasoning Center [6], and a student ambassador for the Yale Admissions Office [7].

My original intent with this website was to create a place where I could help students learn math and econ while sharing ideas that interest me. As I continue to develop this site, feel free reach out with any questions, comments, or concerns (but preferably not with any bills or lawsuits). Please also reach out if you have any special topic requests - I'd love to chat with you. I hope you enjoy!

- Daniel
